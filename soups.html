Soups

Sopa de Estrellas

Ingredients:

1 cup chopped celery

1 cup chopped onion

1 bag of star noodles

2 tbs oil

8 oz can of tomato sauce

2 heaping tbs of chicken knorr

Instructions:

1. in a pan, heat up the oil to medium heat. in a pot, fill the pot up with water and tomato sauce. boil the mixture.

2. prepare the celery and onion

3. add the celery and onion to the pan and cook until onion is clear

4. add in star noodles and stir until golden brown

5. transfer the noodles, onions, and celery to the pot. add a generous amount of chicken knorr to the soup.

6. let the soup boil for 10 minutes and check the noodles periodically

7. once the noodles are cooked, take pot off of heat and serve
